# Customer Satisfaction and Loyalty Dashboard  

### Project Overview  
This Power BI dashboard provides an in-depth analysis of **customer satisfaction and loyalty patterns**. It offers valuable insights into satisfaction scores, loyalty levels, support interactions, and geographical trends, enabling businesses to identify key drivers of customer behavior and improve retention strategies.  

### Key Insights  

#### Growth and Customer Patterns  
The dashboard uncovers diverse satisfaction and loyalty trends across different customer groups. It highlights demographic influences such as **age and gender**, along with purchasing behaviors, to showcase evolving customer expectations.  

#### Service and Support Impact  
Analysis of support interactions reveals how **customer service experiences** directly influence satisfaction levels. It highlights the relationship between contacting support and loyalty classification, uncovering areas for service improvement.  

#### Geographical Distribution  
The interactive map visualizes satisfaction and loyalty across **U.S. cities**, pinpointing regions of higher customer engagement and areas needing attention. This geographical view helps identify local patterns and opportunities.  

#### Satisfaction Factors  
By analyzing reasons behind customer satisfaction (e.g., **Price, Product Variety, Packaging, Support**), the dashboard identifies the most impactful factors shaping customer perceptions and loyalty.  

#### Loyalty Levels  
The breakdown of loyalty into **Low, Medium, and High** categories showcases overall retention health. It also highlights how purchase history and satisfaction drivers contribute to loyalty progression.  

#### Demographic Insights  
The analysis of satisfaction by **age groups and gender** provides deeper understanding of which demographics are most satisfied and loyal. These insights can guide tailored customer engagement strategies.  

#### Segmentation by Group  
Group-wise analysis reveals satisfaction and loyalty differences among customer segments, enabling targeted actions for improving experiences in specific clusters.  

#### Historical and Behavioral Trends  
Although the dataset is static, patterns across satisfaction scores, loyalty levels, and support interactions reflect customer journey behaviors. These trends reveal the long-term impact of service, pricing, and product quality on loyalty.  

### Features  
- **Interactive filters** for customized analysis  
- **Drill-down capabilities** for in-depth insights        
- **Geospatial visualizations** showing customer distribution  
- **Loyalty and support analysis** with satisfaction factor breakdowns

### How to Dive In  
1. Open the `.pbix` file in **Power BI Desktop**  
2. Navigate through the two dashboard pages:     
   - **Overview** – Overall satisfaction, demographics, factors, and geographical insights  
   - **Loyalty and Support Analysis** – Loyalty levels, support interactions, and satisfaction drivers  
3. Use interactive filters to explore different customer groups, locations, or satisfaction factors  
4. Click on visuals to reveal more detailed insights
